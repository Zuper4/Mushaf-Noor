Privacy Policy for Mushaf Noor

Last updated: November 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Mushaf Noor ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Quran reading application.

2. Information We Collect

Mushaf Noor is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

2.1 Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • User Preferences: Dark/light mode, language selection, font size, font family, and translation display settings
  • Reading Progress: Current page, selected Qiraat (recitation style), and Surah list scroll position
  • Downloaded Content: Quran page images and audio files for offline access
  • Database Records: Information about downloaded Qiraats and audio files stored in local SQLite database

3. How We Use Your Information

All information stored by Mushaf Noor remains on your device and is used solely to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Maintain your reading progress
  • Enable offline access to downloaded Quran content
  • Provide a personalized reading experience

4. Data Transmission

The app connects to the internet only to:

  • Download Quran page images (PDFs converted to images)
  • Download audio recitations for different Qiraats

No personal data, usage statistics, or analytics are transmitted to any server.

5. Permissions

Mushaf Noor requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save downloaded Quran pages and audio files for offline access
  • Internet: To download Quran content and audio recitations
  • Notifications: To display playback controls and status when audio plays in the background

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection.

6. Third-Party Services

Mushaf Noor does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics services
  • Advertisement networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting services that transmit data

7. Children's Privacy

Mushaf Noor is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for children using the app.

8. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SharedPreferences for user settings
  • SQLite database for content management
  • Local file storage for downloaded content

Your data is as secure as your device's security measures (device lock, encryption, etc.).

9. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clearing app data through your device's Settings → Apps → Mushaf Noor → Storage → Clear Data

This will permanently remove all downloaded content, preferences, and progress from your device.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Open Source

Mushaf Noor's code is available on GitHub. You can verify our privacy practices by reviewing the source code at: https://github.com/Zuper4/Mushaf-Noor

12. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can access all your data through the app interface
  • You can delete your data at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • Your data is never shared with third parties

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the app's data practices, please contact us:

GitHub: https://github.com/Zuper4/Mushaf-Noor/issues
